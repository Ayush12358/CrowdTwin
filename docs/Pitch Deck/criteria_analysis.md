## Answers Table

| Category | Who | When | What | Where | Why | How |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Simulation** | Event organizers, security leads. | Pre-event planning & real-time. | Fast-forwards crowd behavior (ABM). | Large gatherings, transport hubs. | Bypass reactive lag of CCTV. | Simulations in 10s via live data. |
| **2. Digital Twin** | Stakeholders, control room staff. | During live event execution. | Real-time digital replica of venue. | Control rooms, mobile units. | Single source of truth for flow. | Syncs drone/turnstile with virtual agents. |
| **3. The Problem** | Attendees, Organizers, Insurers. | When density hits >7 p/m². | Crushes caused by delayed detection. | Exits, gates, narrow bottlenecks. | CCTV alerts *after* critical density. | Reactive chains leading to tragedy. |
| **4. Solution** | Risk-averse organizers & insurers. | 15 mins before a potential crush. | Early-warning predictive engine. | On-site Crowd-Twin dashboard. | Prevents tragedy via flow diversion. | Identification of "Future Red Zones." |
| **5. The Market** | Festivals, Airports, Pilgrimages. | High-risk era (Post-Itaewon). | Multi-million $ liability/safety market. | Global (Significant focus on India). | Massive liability costs & regulation. | Subscription-based / Horizontal scale. |
| **6. The Client** | Festival promoters, Insurers. | Planning and critical execution. | Risk reduction & liability mitigation. | Global venues, high-footfall sites. | Avoid negligence & massive claims. | Control room operations & deployment. |
| **7. The Ask** | Attendees, fans, pilgrims. | Vacations, religious/cultural events. | Safe and enjoyable experience. | Stadiums, shrines, open fields. | Social/Cultural engagement. | Following paths, buying tickets. |
| **8. Competition** | MassMotion, WaitTime, CCTV AI. | Design phase (MM) / Real-time (WT). | Offline modeling or peak detection. | Architecture firms, control rooms. | They only look at Past or Present. | Treat crowds as water or pixel density. |
| **9. Present Tech** | Standard security teams. | Reactive phase (post-detection). | CCTV, static heatmaps, turnstiles. | High-traffic corridors and gates. | Low cost but limited by reaction. | Human monitoring / Simple AI triggers. |
| **10. Future Tech** | Crowd-Twin powered venues. | Proactive phase (pre-crush). | Psychological Simulation & Twins. | Smart venues, global gatherings. | Only tech to simulate "panic intent". | Millions of virtual brains in parallel. |
| **11. The Vision** | Global society (Safety first). | Full market saturation. | Elimination of stampede deaths. | Everywhere crowds gather. | Safety is a fundamental human right. | Becoming the standard safety layer. |
